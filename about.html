About Hannah Studios

Hannah Studios is a creative indie software studio where fine art meets clean code. Founded with the belief that utility apps can be beautiful and that digital tools should respect the user, the studio focuses on building iOS experiences that feel distinct, tactile, and personal.

With a background that bridges photography, traditional painting, and software engineering, I approach app development as a craft. Every pixel is considered, and every interaction is polished.

Since its inception, the studio has shipped multiple products including Pan65, a cinematic panoramic camera, and Slideshow Classic. The goal is simple: to create software that I want to use myself, and that empowers others to be creative in their daily lives.

What I do

  • iOS app design & development (SwiftUI & UIKit)
  • App Store launch strategy & ASO
  • Rapid prototyping and interactive design
  • Building creative tools for photographers and artists
